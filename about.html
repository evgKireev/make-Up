ABOUT ME
My name is Valya and for 8 years I have been creating images for cinema, theater and fashion shows.
Among my works:
  • Makeup for client
  • Art Makeup
  • Face painting
  • Hairstyles
  • Makeup for theater and cinema
In my work, I am guided not only by the form, but also by the content. Every detail is important, and the characters which I create have their own story and character.
I have created images for influencers and celebrities. Among them: Daria Kostritskaya, Maksim Pushkin , Alena Melovatskaya, Oksana Kovalevskaya, Iva Sativa.
I am open to suggestions and I am waiting for your messages.
